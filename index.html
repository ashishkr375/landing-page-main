Our Mission

SugarSync is a virtual assistant designed to help people who lack access to medical care, particularly focusing on diabetes management. Users can submit their blood sugar levels or blood pressure data, and the app will track this information, providing personalized diet and exercise recommendations. For visually impaired users, the assistant can communicate using voice technology. The app also informs users about the seriousness of their symptoms and advises when immediate medical assistance is needed.
